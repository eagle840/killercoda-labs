# Introduction to InfluxDB & Time-Series Forecasting

Welcome to this lab! In this guide, you will learn how to:

1. **Deploy InfluxDB** using Docker to store and manage time-series data.
2. **Ingest Data** into InfluxDB using the Influx CLI and Flux tasks.
3. **Analyze & Forecast** trends by integrating InfluxDB with a Jupyter notebook environment, leveraging powerful libraries like **Pandas** and **Prophet** to predict future data points based on historical patterns.

This lab is designed to give you a practical, hands-on understanding of how modern time-series databases power real-world analytics and forecasting pipelines. Let's get started!
